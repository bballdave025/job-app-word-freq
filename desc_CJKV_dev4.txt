JOB DESCRIPTION
The Software Development Engineer 4 is a professional, full-stack engineer lead role responsible for developing technical designs and developing and maintaining software source code to support a solution. As an experienced member of a development team, the individual is encouraged to solve complex technical issues and programming tasks. The individual may be asked to act as a technical lead on projects or solutions with moderate complexity or scope.

This individual works with divine guidance to provide or support technology that furthers the mission of the Church and reflects the eternal impact of the gospel.

RESPONSIBILITIES
Implement plan to support team-wide development strategy
Contribute to and perform analysis of functional requirements
Participate in vendor or technology evaluations and identify critical gaps in their offerings
Participate in the formulation of technical recommendations
Act as a technical lead, and if assigned, mentor less-senior developers
Collaborate with design to provide input, alternatives, and improvements to design concepts
Create technical designs mapped to functional requirements
Build and maintain relationships with cross-functional team leads to promote collaboration
Provide estimates for development tasks with no guidance
Design, develop, and test code for software development projects
Participate in code reviews
Support requirements and design driven processes by partnering with User Experience Professionals.
Advocate for the user experience within their product areas
Participate in the Problem Management process and fix defects
Contribute to the design, and implementation, and measures required to protect solutions from security compromise
Comply with policies, standards, and regulatory requirements and ensure all others are doing the same
Research existing technical knowledge (inside and outside the organization) such as language, tools, patterns, architectures, system design, performance, accessibility, and usability
Effectively document new technical knowledge and train others on emerging needs
Work with Product/Engineering to deliver work as specified and on schedule
