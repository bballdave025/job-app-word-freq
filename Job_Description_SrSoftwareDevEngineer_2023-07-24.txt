JOB DESCRIPTION
Are you an experienced and passionate software engineer seeking an opportunity to make a meaningful impact? Join the exceptional team at FamilySearch.org, the world's largest genealogy organization, and play a crucial role in revolutionizing how people connect with their family history.

About Us: FamilySearch.org is dedicated to preserving and sharing the world's genealogical records, fostering connections among families across generations. As a pioneering industry leader, we are committed to leveraging innovative tools and technologies to empower millions of users worldwide to make remarkable discoveries about their ancestors and family history.

Your Role: As a Senior Software Engineer at FamilySearch.org, you will be at the forefront of our technological advancements, driving the development of robust and scalable software solutions. You will collaborate with a talented team of engineers, designers, and product managers to enhance the FamilySearch.org platform, delivering exceptional user experiences.

RESPONSIBILITIES
Collaborate with cross-functional teams to design, develop, and implement software solutions that align with business objectives and user needs.
Architect and maintain scalable, performant, and secure web-based applications using industry-standard programming languages and frameworks.
Take ownership of projects throughout the entire software development lifecycle, ensuring high-quality and on-time delivery.
Conduct thorough code reviews, provide constructive feedback, and mentor junior engineers to foster a culture of continuous improvement.
Stay abreast of emerging technologies and industry trends, evaluating their potential impact and relevance to our platform.
Solve complex technical challenges and contribute to architectural decisions to drive innovation and optimize system performance.
Communicate effectively with stakeholders, contributing to product strategy and roadmap discussions.
Uphold software engineering best practices, including code quality, documentation, and maintainability.

Why Join FamilySearch.org?
